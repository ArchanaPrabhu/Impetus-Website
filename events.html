Cyborg is a fun-filled technical event and is ideal to students of all branches for participation.

Round 1: WORDSWORTH

A long, scientific word will be provided. Legitimate, smaller words needs to be formed out of it in minimum time. A technical poetry needs to be composed by the teams to proceed to the next round.

Round 2: Situation round A complex, technical situation will be provided to each team. They need to get out of the situation in a very effective manner. The situations will be released on-spot.

Round 3: AirCrash - The teams will be given noticeable personalities. All these famous figures will be in an airplane that is about to crash, the task is to convince the pilot(judge) why they should be given the only parachute available.
