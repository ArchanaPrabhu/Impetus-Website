Vaksamara is a Kannada Debate with an on-stage final round.

Prelims: Will be held on 22nd March, with the topic given 3 days prior to this. Participants may talk for 4+(1) mins.

Finals: Selected participants are divided into two teams for a debate on a certain subject.

Round 1: Participants will be given failed products. They need to find an innovative idea to bring up the product and present the solution.

Round 2: Each team needs to present the solution in the form of mad-ads.

Round 3: The teams will have to give an alternative solution for a realtime company problem. Based on the solution and the way they try to question the otherteams, each team will gain points.

Round 1: General quiz on automobiles.

Round 2:Innovation round

The participants would be given a chart and sketch pens. They are supposed to innovate or upgrade a part of vehicle for better efficiency and less cost/pollution which could be practically possible. The participants are supposed to put the theory into a graph or a design and they have to present it to the judges after which there will be a questionnaire by the judges.

Round 3: It is a rapid fire question on automation and automobiles.

Round 1: It is an elimination round where a crossword will be given and the teams will have to fill that.

Only 8 teams will be sekected for the next round.

Before the round starts, the teams will be given a problem statement acc. to which they have to go ahead.

Round 2: In this round the teams will be given a Boolean expression and they have to solve it. In the end they will get a simple equation which will lead to a place and this marks the beginning of the treasure hunt. At the end all the teams will have components to rig up the circuit.

Round 3: The teams have to rig up circuits according to the problem statement given and in the end they have to explain how they did that and the winner will be announced.
